Abstract

The Airline Passenger Review Ontology is a proposed solution for the evaluation (PEC2) of the course "Representacion del Conocimiento" imparted at the UOC (Universitat Oberta de Catalunya).

Airline Passenger Review: Description back to ToC

This ontology defines concepts for the representation of customer satisfaction reviews by airline passengers. Created by Xema Belmonte Marin (jbelmontemari@uoc.edu)
